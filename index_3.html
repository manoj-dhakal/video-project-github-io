About the Video

Get ready to embark on a laughter-filled escapade with "SOS-UH 101: Unleash the Power Within: A Crash Course to Excel, Thrive, and Make the Best of Your Life at New York University Abu Dhabi." This interactive video combines comedy and education to guide students on a transformative journey. Join our protagonists as they learn the art of mischief, explore hypnosis to uncover the truth, and discover the secrets of being a good student. With a captivating lecture by Professor Ameera and scenes featuring dedicated student Abi, this interactive film brings college life at NYU Abu Dhabi to life.

Excel, thrive, and make the best of your life.
